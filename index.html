Making air travel
extraordinary.

Uniquely designed individual pods will create a unique environment for guests to enjoy our in-flight entertainment. Each seat will transform into an immersive pod comprised of LED screens encapsulating the passenger for complete privacy. Once the immersive experience is complete, the pod transforms back to its regular seat so the guest can enjoy the other perks on the plane.

where to begin

An informative onboarding video will play at the beginning to make the user experience easier for travelers. The pod's user interface will be designed with the ability to call flight attendants, visualize route maps, display food and beverage menus, and play a variety of immersive videos for users to explore.

experience

The pod's surrounding screens are safer than VR headsets to avoid the negative side effects of having the virtual world mere inches away from the user's face but still allow guests to be fully immersed in the experience of their choosing.

your controls

After ascension, the pod experiences can be accessed. Optical hand tracking adds freedom of motion in addition to tradition touchpad control.
